About Our Organization

Our goal at Med Connect is to help facilitate helpful communication between patients, doctors, and organizations. Whether it be an organization organizing an event, a doctor administering a survey, or a patient just asking questions, we believe that open and quick communication is vital to both the wellbeing of patients and the effectiveness of doctors. Check out the 'Events' page to find nearby activities to sign up for, and 'Surveys' to help give a doctor meaninful feedback regarding how patients take care of themselves.
